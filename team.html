Who We Are

The People Behind the Pixels

We're a tight-knit crew of artists and technicians who believe the best work grows in a place where creativity is nurtured and everyone keeps learning.

A studio built like a garden

Our name says it: a little "hide and seek," a lot of "seeds." We give ideas — and people — room to grow. Junior artists work shoulder to shoulder with leads, and every project is a chance to sharpen the craft.

From our home base in Puchong, Malaysia, we collaborate across animation, VFX, game art and post to deliver cinematic work for brands and studios around the region and beyond.
